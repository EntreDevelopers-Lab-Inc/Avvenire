ERC721A

First implemented in the Azuki mint, ERC721A enables the minting of multiple NFTs for the same gas cost of a single NFT.

Avvenire employs the same benefits gained in the Azuki mint, as we have developed with their new protocol. These innovations include the removal of duplicate storage and batch updating. ERC721A optimizes data storage by leveraging string concatentation at the time of reading the NFT instead of at the time of writing (mint). Since users are unlikely to pay for read operations, they reap the gas benefits. Furthermore, the use of batch updating allows ERC721A mints update the owner's balance once per transaction instead of once per token.

Tradeable Traits

Our team views NFTs as digital embodiments of real-life objects. For example, an Avvenire Citizen should just be a Web3 person.

Consider any person. Over the course of one week, this individual will likely wear a variety of clothing and jewelry. Over the course of a year, they may change their hairstyle or get a tattoo. We believe that Avvenire Citizens should have the same capabilities. In addition to making these traits interchangeable, we decided that each individual trait should be tradable. These can be listed on OpenSea or LooksRare and traded while not bound to an Avvenire Citizen. We look forward to giving Avvenire Citizens access to this new paradigm of Web3.
