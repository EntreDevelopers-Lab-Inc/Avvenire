Welcome to the Avvenire Project

Based in the near future, the Avvenire City is a utopia whereby the love for art and community are in synchronization. You, as a holder of land in Avvenire City are part of an exclusive community whereby various benefits are given to land holders via airdrops and new offerings. The world of Avvenire City is expanding, and we want you to be part of the journey. Let’s build the brand of Avvenire together.

Roadmap Explore Avvenire City

Roadmap

  • Phase 1: Citizenship Mint
    Launch of the Avvenire Citizenship Mint (limited to 3,000). Welcome to all official founding citizens of Avvenire City. As a founding citizen member, you are not only part of something extraordinary, but you also have the ability to contribute to the further development of Avvenire City.
    Models are based around various types of commercial real estate in RL: Retail Shops, Laboratories, Museums, Film Studio, Cafe, Restaurant, Mini Golf etc.
  • Phase 2: Avvenire City Parcel Release
    Founding citizens will be airdropped a part of Avvenire City, called an Avvenire Parcel. We’ve uniquely designed the parcel to suit the personality of your citizenship. The Avvenire Parcels give access to the Avvenire metaverse utility.
  • Phase 3: Avvenire City Exclusive Clothing Apparel
    Avvenire City is not only a community of like minded individuals, but also a brand. Avvenire Citizens will be given the exclusive opportunity to purchase apparel designed and inspired by the Avvenire Citizen collection.
  • Phase 4: Metaverse Expansion of Avvenire City
    The most suitable metaverse platform will be chosen for the development of Avvenire City in the metaverse. Holders of either the parcel or citizenship nft will gain specific access and ownership to the Avvenire City Metaverse.
  • Phase 5: The future, to be continued...
    The opportunities are endless for Avvenire, as we expand out into both the physical and web3 worlds. From here, we want you to be a part of the decision making in what we do next. Whether that be in-real-life parties, apparel brand expansion or further airdrops, the future of Avvenire City is in your hands.
